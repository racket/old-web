At the PLT Meeting of February 11,1998, we discussed compatibility issues of successive DrScheme releases. Currently, releases are numbered sequentially, with no distinctions in status among them. Because we plan to revise MrEd API's and MzScheme features substantially after the 52 release, it becomes clear that releases in fact enjoy different status. As is common in commercial and other software, after the 52 release, we will make those distinctions apparent. Specifically:
  • There will be major releases, sent out at intervals measured in years
  • There will be minor releases of major release versions
  • Version numbering may take the form of <major>.<minor> or similar notation
  • Within major releases, versions should be backward compatible with minimal disruption to API's
